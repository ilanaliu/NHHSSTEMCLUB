At Home Science Activities!

Brain Teasers

Brain teasers are fun, thought-provoking puzzles that challenge students to think critically and creatively. They spark curiosity and encourage problem-solving skills in a collaborative and engaging way.
Brain Games

Online brain games are interactive tools that help sharpen memory, logic, and attention through fun digital challenges. They make learning engaging while boosting cognitive skills in a tech-friendly format.
About the Brain

On Khan Academy, we explore how the brain controls everything from movement to memory, acting as the command center of the body. Learning about the brain helps students understand neuroscience and the science behind how we think, learn, and feel.
Our Body

Through Khan Academy, we learn how the human body works as a complex system made up of organs, muscles, and cells working together. Understanding the body helps students appreciate biology, health, and the science behind how we live and grow.
Germs?

Khan Academy can teach students about germs—tiny organisms like bacteria and viruses that can make us sick. Learning how germs spread helps promote good hygiene and awareness of how science keeps us healthy.
Body Fun!

Siemen's body-themed online game help students explore anatomy in an interactive and fun way. These games let them learn about organs, systems, and body functions through quizzes, simulations, and virtual dissections.
Math Games

Math games turn numbers into fun by using puzzles, challenges, and logic-based activities to boost problem-solving skills. They help students build confidence and enjoy learning math through play and friendly competition.
Math Lessons

Math lessons from high school tutors to provide personalized support and in-depth explanations on topics like algebra, arithmetics, and geometry. These lessons help students strengthen their math skills through guided practice and one-on-one tutoring.

Coding Games

Coding games make learning to program exciting by turning coding challenges into interactive adventures. They help students build logic and computational thinking skills while having fun creating and solving puzzles.
Learn to code

Coding lessons from Khan Academy introduce students to programming through fun, beginner-friendly tutorials. These interactive lessons teach the basics of JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and problem-solving through creative coding projects.
Coding Toys

Coding toys like programmable robots and interactive kits make learning to code fun and hands-on. These toys allow students to create simple programs, solve challenges, and develop critical thinking skills while exploring the world of programming.
Space & Planets

xExplore the wonders of space and planets, from the mysteries of distant galaxies to the characteristics of planets in our solar system. Learning about space sparks curiosity about astronomy, physics, and the vast universe beyond Earth.
Our Earth

Study Earth as a dynamic planet, focusing on its geology, atmosphere, ecosystems, and natural processes. Understanding Earth helps students appreciate environmental science and the importance of protecting our planet for future generations.

Our Process

  • Identify and Organize

    We recognized a lack of STEM outreach in local elementary and middle schools and proposed creating a student-led initiative to bridge that gap. We recruited passionate high school volunteers and secured approval from school administrators.

  • Design and Launch Programs

    We developed engaging science lessons, hands-on experiments, and digital resources tailored to younger students. Then, we coordinated regular visits to each school, ensuring consistent programming and enthusiastic participation.

  • Expand and Sustain

    To keep the club growing, we built a website to share updates, resources, and success stories. We also established leadership roles and a training system so the club could continue after we graduate.
